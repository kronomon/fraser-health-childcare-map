Why was this website created?

When we were searching for childcare facilities for our daughter, we found the process a bit difficult to navigate.

  • Some daycares were not easily searchable online
  • Google Maps was missing some licensed facilities
  • Hard to compare facilities (many were lacking reviews/ratings)
  • Fraser Health website hard to navigate when comparing facilities near us
I built this tool to help us find facilities nearby and easily navigate to the Fraser Health website. We hope this website will help other families too!

How do I use this tool?

Our process for searching daycares was:

  1. Look for licensed childcare facilities near us using this map
  2. Check Fraser Health's inspection info and search Google for more information
  3. Apply to the facilities we liked

No childcare centres are shown near me. Why?

This website's data is fetched from the Fraser Health region. You are likely in a different region.

Please check these links for other health regions in BC:

  1. Vancouver Coastal Health: Vancouver Coastal Health Inspection Reports
  2. All other health regions: BC Government Childcare Map

Please review the BC government website on different types of Child Care

How does this tool work?

  1. Data is pulled from the Fraser Health Childcare database
  2. Childcare facilities are displayed on a map via Google Maps API for your viewing pleasure

The data is periodically updated. Please let me know if you notice any missing facilities
